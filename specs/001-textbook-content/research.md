# Research: Physical AI Textbook Content

## Overview
This document captures the research and literature review for the physical AI textbook content.

## Key Research Areas

### 1. Physical AI Fundamentals
- Definition and scope of physical AI
- Core principles of physics-based machine learning
- Differences from traditional AI approaches

### 2. Mathematical Foundations
- Newtonian mechanics and AI applications
- Differential equations in physical modeling
- Probability and uncertainty in physical systems

### 3. Simulation Techniques
- Physics engines and their integration with AI
- Numerical methods for physical simulation
- Real-time vs. offline simulation considerations

### 4. Applications
- Robotics and embodied AI
- Computer graphics and animation
- Scientific computing and modeling
- Industrial automation
